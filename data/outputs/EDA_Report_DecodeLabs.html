08 —

Key Findings & Recommendations

F1
41.4% non-fulfilment rate is the #1 business problem. With 20.8% Cancelled and 20.6% Returned, only 1 in 5 orders successfully delivers. Root cause analysis on cancellation reasons (out-of-stock? checkout friction? payment failure?) is the highest-priority action.
F2
UnitPrice is the primary revenue lever. Correlation of 0.72 vs Quantity's 0.62 means premium product positioning and upselling to higher-value SKUs will outperform volume-growth strategies.
F3
Coupons are not converting to higher spend. The $15 difference in avg order value between coupon and non-coupon users is statistically negligible. Coupon strategy should be restructured — perhaps use them only for re-activation of lapsed customers rather than general distribution.
F4
Chair & Printer are underrated revenue engines. Both surpass Laptop in total revenue despite lower avg order value — their higher order volumes make them critical. Supply chain and inventory prioritisation should reflect this.
F5
7 high-value outlier orders (>$3,335) are VIP signals. These bulk/premium orders should not be removed or treated as errors. Investigate whether they represent a B2B segment that could be nurtured with dedicated account management.
